What is Needed

Transportation is needed every Sunday to bring African friends and members of the church to and from Sunday meetings. Volunteer drivers are generally asked to provide rides for 1 or 2 households.

Meeting Start: 12:30 PM

Meeting End: 2:30 PM

Location: 3229 Bogus Basin Road, Boise, Idaho

Note: A potluck dinner is held every Sunday right after church, so count on leaving 30 minutes or so after meetings end.

How the Process Works

  1. Assignments with instructions are made and communicated by text every Saturday evening. Driving needs are always in flux, so permanent assignments are generally not possible.
  2. Drivers are generally asked to make 1 or 2 stops. Standard protocols are observed in terms of male-female pairing and minors accompaniment.
  3. You can sign up to drive round-trip or just one-way. All volunteers are welcome, but round-trip availability is especially helpful.
  4. You'll receive an automated text message with a link to update your availability each Thursday evening at 7:00 PM.
  5. When updating your status, please ensure you're logged in under the Google account associated with your original sign-up email to avoid duplicate records.
  6. For communication with French or Swahili-speaking friends, Google Translate or AI assistants can be helpful, or reach out to the assigned missionaries for assistance.

Questions? Contact Brother Chuck VanDam at 208-995-3720
